Decentralized Clinic welcomes you

Decentralized currency has evolved beyond a mere monetary project; it has introduced a new concept of decentralizing systems not vulnerable to outside influences, such as governments and big businesses. Here, we aim to pioneer the concept of a decentralized clinic, a facility where individuals can access necessary healthcare services. This platform will offer global access to medical professionals regardless of geographical location. Variances in regulatory frameworks pose challenges to establishing such a platform on a worldwide scale, hence our belief in the decentralized clinic as a solution. Our top priority is providing access to quality medical advice. In parallel, we plan to launch our native token, based on the TON network. Stay tuned for updates!
